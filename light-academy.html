Our Story

Planting Seeds of Opportunity

Founded in 2013 by Rev. Dr. Tailey and Pst. Mrs Agnes W. Garley, Light Academy Model School System was born from a deep compassion for the orphaned and impoverished children of Liberia. We believe that every child deserves the chance to learn, grow, and build a brighter future, regardless of their circumstances.

Our Mission

To provide free, quality education and holistic support to vulnerable children in Liberia, empowering them to become productive and responsible citizens.

Our Vission

To see a Liberia where every child has the opportunity to reach their full potential through education and care.

Our Impact

The Difference We Make

From humble beginnings in a small shack, Light Academy now proudly serves over 900 students, providing them with a safe and nurturing learning environment. We are committed to breaking the cycle of poverty and empowering the next generation of Liberian leaders.
