About Us!

Building a Skilled Workforce for Liberia

Founded by Rev. Tailey W. Garley, the MKS Vocational and Technical Institute is committed to equipping Liberians with practical, in-demand skills to drive economic growth and individual success. We are proud to offer high-quality vocational training completely free of tuition.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, high-quality, and tuition-free vocational and technical training that empowers individuals with the skills needed for sustainable employment and economic development in Liberia.

Our Vission

To be a leading vocational and technical training institution in Liberia, contributing significantly to a skilled and productive workforce.

Our Impact

Transforming Lives Through Skills Development

By providing accessible vocational training, MKS Institute is empowering individuals to secure meaningful employment and contribute to the development of their communities. We are proud to collaborate with Light Academy Model School System in this vital mission.

Partnering for Holistic Development

MKS Vocational and Technical Institute is proud to collaborate with Light Academy Model School System, founded by the same visionary leader, Rev. Tailey W. Garley. We jointly organize career fairs and provide vocational training opportunities to Light Academy students, offering them pathways to both academic and technical careers.
