The University

At Faith University College Liberia, we are committed to academic excellence, character development, and leadership training. Our institution provides a transformative learning experience that empowers students with knowledge, skills, and values to excel in their careers and contribute positively to society.

At Faith University College Liberia, we are committed to academic excellence, character development, and leadership training. Our institution provides a transformative learning experience that empowers students with knowledge, skills, and values to excel in their careers and contribute positively to society.

Our Mission

To provide quality and accessible education that nurtures intellectual growth, professional competence, and moral integrity, equipping students with the knowledge and skills to lead and serve their communities effectively.

Our Vission

To be a center of excellence in higher education, recognized for producing competent, innovative, and socially responsible graduates who drive positive change in Liberia and beyond.

Our Objectives

Our objectives as a university are to:

1.

Provide facilities for learning and give instructions and training in such areas of knowledge that will produce sound and mentally equipped graduates, who will provide intellectual leadership in academic institutions, industry, and the public sector through the Total Man Concept approach;

2.

Develop and offer academic and professional programmes leading to the award of diplomas, first degrees, and higher degrees, which emphasize planning, adaptive and technological maintenance, developmental and productive skills; promote by research and other means, the advancement of knowledge and its practical;

3.

Application to social, cultural, economic, scientific, and technological problems; Encourage and promote scholarship and conduct research in all fields of learning and human endeavour;

4.

Disseminate scientific and technological knowledge among scientists, researchers, industries, trade services, and other bodies; and

5.

Relate its activities to Nigeria's technological, scientific, and socio-economic needs and undertake other activities appropriate for a university of the highest standard.

Our Core Values

Our core values are the defining components of the Faith University vision. They reflect our beliefs in the encrypted truths that firmly define our purpose and the underlining ethos of our existence as a University.

We nurture a Christ-centered community where spiritual growth is foundational to academic and personal excellence. Our commitment to prayer, biblical values, and faith in God guides all that we do.

We believe that with faith, vision, and determination, all things are possible. We empower our students to think beyond limitations and embrace opportunities with courage and confidence.

We are dedicated to equipping students with knowledge, skills, character to become agents of transformation. Through innovative learning anf mentorship, we foster growth for lifelong impact.

We uphold honesty, transparency, and strong moral principles in all areas of life. Integrity is the bedrock of our actions, relationships, and leadership.

We embrace personal and collective accountability. At Faith University College, students and staff are encouraged to act with a sense of duty toward themselves, their community, and the nation.

We promote a culture of hard work, focus, and excellence. We believe conssitent effort and a disciplined spirit are key to achieving purpose and fulfilling destiny.

We honor the value of selfless service and commitment. We teach our community to give their best (even when it costs) to achieve greater good and fulfill divine purpose.
